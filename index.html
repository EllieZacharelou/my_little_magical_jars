About Me

I am Ellie Z. and since 2020 I have designed my own collection of cute but still romantic pendant necklaces.
Things I love are the sea in the summer, lavender fields, chocolate deserts, occasion dresses, lettering and exploring different parts of Italy.

I draw my inspiration from purple and fuchsia flowers, the autumn leaves, the romantic period's fashion style, disney-characters, pastel colours, the seaside & more...
My little jars are uniquely decorated with love and care!
